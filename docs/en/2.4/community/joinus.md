# Join Us

The Konado Project development group is a community-driven open-source team under the [Godot Hub](https://godothub.com) community. We are committed to the development, maintenance, and continued growth of Konado. Developers interested in Konado are welcome to join us, improve and innovate the project together, and contribute to the open-source community.
