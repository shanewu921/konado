# Peripheral IP Introduction

## About Kona

Kona is Konado's dedicated mascot. Built around the image of a **lively, adorable, and energetic** girl, she is not only an important part of the brand's visual identity, but also a friendly bridge between users and Konado.

## Kona's Design

Kona appears throughout Konado's experience. Whether on the startup screen, the official website homepage, or the product documentation, her presence brings vitality to the brand. As Konado evolves across versions, Kona's design evolves with it: matching the style of each new release while becoming more refined and recognizable through careful detail work.

Konado usually introduces a new exclusive Kona design during **major version updates**. Each design has its own unique ideas, preserving her core personality while adding more charm and memorable details to the product.

Kona may also receive smaller changes in minor releases, such as seasonal outfits or additional accessories, making her design richer and more varied.
